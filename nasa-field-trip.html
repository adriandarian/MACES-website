Every year, the Center organizes NASA field trip. 40-50 high school students along with MACES undergraduate fellows will attend. During the 5-hr tour at NASA Ames, students will visit Research and Testing Facilities such as Wind Tunnel, Flight Simulator and 20-G Centrifuge (https://www.nasa.gov/ames-partnerships/opportunities/facilities). In addition to expose to a variety of research activities through presentations by NASA researchers, students will have the rare opportunity to interact with scientists and engineers at NASA.
